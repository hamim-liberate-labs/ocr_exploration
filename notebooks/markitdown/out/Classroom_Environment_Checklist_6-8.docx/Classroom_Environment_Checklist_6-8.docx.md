Bulletin Boards (Classroom and Hallway)
All backing paper must be replaced for the 2024-2025 school
year.
Designated sections for each content area, clearly labeled, with
authentic student work of varying levels (at least 10 student samples)
and meaningful feedback based on a rubric.
Feedback should include a specific next step and a strategy for getting
there, as well as one area of celebration, both based on the rubric.
Bulletin Boards should include: Teacher name(s), Date, Standards
addressed, Task, Rubric, Student work with feedback.
Classroom Library/Resource Center (labeled as such)
All bins/books should be easily accessible to students, and bins should
be maintained and kept tidy. All ELA/ENL rooms must have a clearly
labeled library with books organized by genre. All other contents
should have content specific libraries/resource centers.
Safety
● Visible and accessible Red Emergency Folder (includes
Assembly Cards) and updated student rosters
● Allergy notification system (ie. Nut Free Zone sign provided by
Admin) as needed
Additional Classroom Expectations:
● Food and Drinks: No glass bottles are permitted in the school
building. If you permit students to eat in the classroom, ensure
that all trash is properly removed.
● Outer Clothing: Make sure students have removed their hoods,
caps, hats and jackets when they are in your room. If students
are cold, they are permitted to wear long sleeves under their
uniform shirts.
● School Bags: Make sure that all school bags have been
removed and placed on the back of students’ chairs.
● Sports Equipment: Sports equipment is not allowed to be
visible in the classroom or hallways during instructional time.
If sports equipment becomes a distraction to teaching and
learning it must be taken from the student.

●  Cell Phones: Phones are collected in the morning and  returned
at the end of the school day. They are not allowed in the
classroom.
