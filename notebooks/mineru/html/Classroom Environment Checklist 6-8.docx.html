Additional Classroom Expectations:Food and Drinks: No glass bottles are permitted in the schoolbuilding. If you permit students to eat in the classroom, ensurethat all trash is properly removed.Outer Clothing: Make sure students have removed their hoods,caps, hats and jackets when they are in your room. If studentsare cold, they are permitted to wear long sleeves under theiruniform shirts.School Bags: Make sure that all school bags have beenremoved and placed on the back of students' chairs.Sports Equipment: Sports equipment is not allowed to bevisible in the classroom or hallways during instructional timeIf sports equipment becomes a distraction to teaching andlearning it must be taken from the student.
