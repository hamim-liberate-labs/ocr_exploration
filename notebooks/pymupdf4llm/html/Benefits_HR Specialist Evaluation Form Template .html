EMPLOYEE BENEFITS/HR SPECIALIST EVALUATION

I. BENEFITS ADMINISTRATION: STAFF Job Description Elements: Standard: Serve as the benefits expert for Peak to Peak, providing accurate and effective employee benefit coordination for staff

  • Coordinate and distribute materials for benefit orientations, open enrollment and other applicable employee benefit sessions

  • Keep staff abreast of all benefit informational sessions (held both at P2P and BVSD) and open enrollment periods

  • Assist staff with benefit renewal processes, providing guidance and expertise as appropriate

  • Initiate all benefit related paperwork and arrange for completion and submission ensuring deadlines are met

  • Ensure all BVSD medical and dental benefit changes and paperwork are provided to BVSD on time and accurately

  • Conduct staff benefit meetings and lead benefit task force groups as needed

  • Serve as primary contact for all staff benefit inquiries and complaints to ensure prompt, equitable and courteous assistance

  • Manage all short-term and long-term disability claims, ensuring all required paperwork is accurately submitted and salaries are calculated and managed according to plan design

  • Work closely with HR Director on all FMLA cases

  • Support employees with workers compensation claims, ensuring timely and accurate notification to worker’s compensation insurance carrier, employee claim processing, and return to work accommodations and adjustments as needed

II. BENEFITS ADMINISTRATION: ORGANIZATION

Job Description Elements:

Standard: Stay abreast of employer benefit related local and national legislative changes impacting the organization to accurately and effectively coordinate employee benefits for the organization

  • Maintain current knowledge of state and federal legislative updates that impact the organization (i.e. annual maximum Flex contributions, retirement contribution limits, taxable fringe benefit, Healthcare Reform, etc.)

  • Analyze benefit options and plans, including but not limited to reviewing benefit plan design, cost, renewal and ROI to report to Human Resources Director

  • Coordinate human resource procedures to initiate benefits including PERA, disability, life, health insurance and retirement plans, etc.

  • Manage and carryout daily benefit processing such as COBRA, terminations, changes, beneficiaries, disability, accident and death claims, retirement plan rollovers

  • Collect and provide census information for all benefit plans (dental, life, AD&D, STD, LTD, PERA, COBRA, Section 125, retirement plans)

  • Conduct non-discrimination testing on Section 125 plan annually

  • As needed, assist Accounts Payable/Payroll Specialist with monthly reports to be uploaded to retirement plan sites and complete ACH transfers for PERA, PERA 401(k), PERA 457, AXA 403(b)

  • Ensure receipt and processing of benefit premium payments from staff out on FMLA, short- and long-term disability

  • Work closely with accountant and Accounts Payable/Payroll Specialist on necessary reporting for all group insurance policies and maintain statistical data relative to premiums, claims and costs

  • Maintain legally compliant employee benefit files in accordance with school HR practices

  • Initiate monthly payment check requests for benefit invoices, provide accurate GL coding of premiums for accounting system import

  • Review and reconcile monthly benefit premium statements and invoices ensuring that accurate monthly liabilities are reported to accountant

III. BENEFIT RELATED PAYROLL ADMINISTRATION

Job Description Elements:
Standard: Ensure timely and accurate benefit related payroll entries and reporting

  • Compile and maintain all benefit-related payroll deductions for monthly payroll processing

  • Enter all benefit premium deduction changes into Paycom at the start of each FY and each month thereafter prior to payroll processing

  • Assist Accounts Payable/Payroll Specialist and Human Resources Director with monthly payroll process as needed (year-end W-2 processing, monthly PERA reporting, health care reform reporting, etc.)

  • Maintain payroll systems to achieve alignment with HR benefits and other related payroll deductions to ensure effective accounting support

  • Maintain benefit provider payroll web interface and ensure EDI carrier connections are accurate

  • Accurately compile and provide monthly reports to the accountant, human resource director, and district staff for benefit payroll liabilities (i.e. benefit deductions, PERA contributions, 401(k) and 403(b) contributions, FSA deductions, etc.)

  • Assist in preparation of compensation plan and other reports with Human Resource Director’s direct supervision

  • Assist Human Resource Director with yearly employment offer process

  • Assist Human Resource Director with completion of verification of employment requests
